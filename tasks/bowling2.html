Fall 2016: Question 15 (use C language to write this program)

Xiao Ming goes to the bowling alley for another round of bowling, this time with the following rules:

  1. There are ten frames in a game, and each frame has 10 pins. One point is awarded for every pin knocked down; if 10 pins are knocked down then all pins are knocked down.
  2. The maximum score per frame is 10, and a player can roll the ball twice in each frame.
  3. During the first 9 frames, if all pins are knocked down in one hit, then the player gets a STRIKE. The player can add the total number of pins knocked down in the next two rolls into the score.

    In the above example, the player gets a total of 10 + 5 + 2 = 17 points for Frame 1.
  4. During the first 9 frames, if all pins are knocked down in two hits, then the player gets a SPARE. The player can add the total number of pins knocked down in the next two rolls into the score.

    In the above example, the player gets a total of 7 + 3 + 5 = 15 points for Frame 1.
  5. If the player gets a strike in the 10th frame, then the player can roll two more balls, and the score for the extra rolls will be counted as well.
  6. If the player gets a spare in the 10th frame, then the player can roll one more ball, and the score for the extra roll will be counted as well.
  7. The bonus scores earned from strikes or spares can only be applied in the first 9 frames, and CANNOT be applied in the 10th frame.
  8. The scoreboard has 21 columns in total; the first 9 frames have two columns each and the 10th frame has three.

Input instruction: Input 21 whole numbers.

Output instruction: Output the total score.
