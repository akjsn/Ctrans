Electricity Bill

Fall 2018: Question 17 (use Python to make this program)
Fall 2017: Question 16 (use C language to make this program)

Insert the month number, and the amount of electricity consumed in the previous year and in this year (both in kWh). Calculate the total amount to be paid based on the following table.

If you consume less electricity this month compared to the same month in the previous year, you will get a discount of $0.60 for every kWh of electricity you save.

Input instruction: Input the following in order:

  1. Month number
  2. Electricity consumed in the aforementioned month last year
  3. Electricity consumed in the aforementioned month this year

Output instruction: Output the total amount to be paid.

Sample Input Expected Output
7
505
525 		2609.25
7
550
525 		2594.25
