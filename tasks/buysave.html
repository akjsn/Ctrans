Buy More, Save More

Fall 2018: Question 9 (use Python to write this program)
Fall 2017: Question 9 (use C language to write this program)

The prices of three books A, B, and C as well as its discounts are shown as follows: If a customer buys x copies of A, y copies of B, and z copies of C (please input x, y, z), then how much will the customer have to pay in total?

For example: If a customer buys 6 copies of Book A, 12 copies of Book B, and 30 copies of Book C, then, the customer will have to pay:
= 6 * 380 + 12 * 1200 * 0.95 + 30 * 180 * 0.8
= 2,280 + 13,680 + 4,320
= 20,280

Input instruction: Number of copies of book A, B, and C

Output instruction: Output the total price.

Sample Input Expected Output
6
12
30 		20280
