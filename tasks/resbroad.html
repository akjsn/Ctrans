Checking The Results Broadsheet

Spring 2018: Question 20 (use Python to write this program)

A university has just released the results of a recent examination. One of the instructors would like to know who scored above the given standard in a subject, or in the total score.

Input instructions:

  1. First, input N, which represents the number of students (100 > N > 2).
  2. Then, input the students’ names, separated with a space in between.
  3. Input the scores of the above students for Chinese (國文), English (英文), Mathematics (數學), Social Science (社會), and Natural Science (自然), separated with a space in between. Scores should be in the range of 1 to 15.
  4. Inputs in (2) and (3) must be stored in zip format.
  5. Lastly, input the subject name (in Chinese) and the passing grade, separated with a space in between. Input 國文 for Chinese, 英文 for English, 數學 for Mathematics, 社會 for Social Science, and 自然 for Natural Science. Input 總級分 to search for the total grade.

Output instructions:

  1. Output the names of students who scored above the passing grade given in the input, with the names of students separated by a space in between.
  2. If there are no students who score above the passing grade, output “NONE”.

Sample Input Expected Output
3
Lucas Alex Candy
15 13 8
15 12 14
15 13 10
15 12 11
15 12 13
國文 12 		Lucas Alex
