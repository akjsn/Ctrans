Calculating The Final Grade and Rank

Spring 2019: Question 4 (use C language to write this program)

Write a program to calculate the final grade of each student, and determine the ranking of each student.

Input instructions:

  1. First, input the number of students.
  2. For each line afterwards, input the student data in the following order, with each piece of information separated with a space:
    ID, Name, three scores

Output instructions:

  1. First, output the student rankings as follows, with each piece of information separated with a space:
    ID, Name, three scores, total score, rank
  2. Next, output the following in order, with each piece of information shown on a new line:
    Average of three scores (in 3 lines, 1 subject for 1 score), average of total score, median of total score, variance of total score, standard deviation of total score, average of total scores above the median, average of total scores below the median
    Output these data rounded up to 2 decimal places.
