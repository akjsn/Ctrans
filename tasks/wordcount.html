Word Count

Spring 2019: Question 7 (use C language to write this program)

Write a program to count the frequency of words appearing in a sentence.

Input instruction: Input a sentence.

Output instructions:

  1. Output, in lowercase, the frequency of every word in order of its first appearance.
    Each line shows the word, and how many times the word appears in the sentence, separated with a space.
  2. Capitalization of the word in the sentence does not matter.

Sample Input Expected Output
Hi Siri hello siRi world hellO hi 1
siri 2
hello 2
world 1

Good Luck!
